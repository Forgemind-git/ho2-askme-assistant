The pattern

How every sample works

Each is a single-file artifact that hardcodes your real material and calls window.claude.complete() to answer live — no server, no key.

Build your own in Claude

  1. In Claude.ai, turn on Settings → Feature preview → "Create AI-powered artifacts" (one time).
  2. Open any sample's README, copy its example prompt, and paste it into a new Claude chat with your own details filled in.
  3. Claude builds the page as an Artifact. Refine it in the chat until it sounds like you.
  4. Click Publish to get a shareable link. Paste that link into the sample README where it says [paste your published artifact share link here].
  5. Anyone with a Claude account can now open the link and ask your page questions live.
Good to know: Anthropic changed how window.claude.complete behaves on 2025-07-31. If live answers stop working, open your artifact inside claude.ai and confirm the current way to call Claude — the feature may have shifted since these examples were written. No API key is ever required for the course.
