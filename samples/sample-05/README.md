# HO2 Sample 5 — Career Changer

## What you'll build
A page that translates your old career into the language of the job you actually want. It
maps your transferable skills, names your target role, tells your story, and uses an
**"Ask Me Anything"** section to answer the "why should we hire you?" question convincingly.
This folder already contains a finished example (Daniel Reyes, a teacher moving into UX
research) — open it, see it working, then make it yours.

## Use it with your Claude.ai subscription
No API key needed. Just your normal Claude.ai login.

1. Open **`index.html`** from this folder in your browser to see the finished example page.
2. Open **Claude.ai** in another tab (your subscription — no API key, no billing).
3. Paste **the example prompt below** and fill in your old roles and your target role.
   Claude is great at reframing past experience for a new audience — that's the hard part,
   and it does it well.
4. Open `index.html` in a text editor, replace the example text with what Claude wrote,
   and save.
5. Refresh the page in your browser to see your version.
6. (Optional) For a live link, push to GitHub and turn on **Settings → Pages** on `main`.

## The example prompt
Copy this into Claude.ai and fill in the parts in CAPITALS:

```
I'm changing careers and need help reframing my old experience for a new field. Help me write the content for a one-page site.

About my switch:
- Name: YOUR NAME
- The career I'm leaving: e.g. 8 years as a secondary-school teacher
- The role I'm moving into: e.g. UX Researcher
- Things I did in my old job that might transfer: LIST A FEW

Please give me:
1. A short tagline showing the switch, e.g. "Teacher → UX Researcher".
2. Four transferable skills. For each: the skill name, where it came from in my old career, and one sentence on how it applies to my target role.
3. A short paragraph on why I'm a strong fit for the new role.
4. Convincing answers to these three hiring-manager questions, two or three sentences each:
   - "Why are you qualified for this role without direct experience?"
   - "Why are you making this switch?"
   - "How have you prepared for this new field?"

Translate my experience into the new field's language — focus on what I can do, not my old job titles.
```

## Make it your own
- Replace the four transferable skills with the ones that best fit your target role.
- Rewrite the story in your own voice — honesty about the switch reads as confidence.
- Change the colour by editing `--color` near the top of `index.html`.
