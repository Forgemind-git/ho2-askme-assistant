About

I spent 7 years as a secondary school science teacher, and I'm now moving into UX research. Here's the honest bridge: teaching is really the daily practice of watching real people struggle with something, finding where they get stuck, and redesigning until it clicks. That's user research. I've since completed a UX research course and run three volunteer projects to prove it in the field. I'm still building my quantitative and analytics depth — and I'd rather say that plainly than pretend otherwise.
