About Me

How I use AI in my product work

I'm a Product Manager at a Series A fintech startup, responsible for our payments and onboarding products. AI has fundamentally changed how fast I can go from idea to spec to stakeholder alignment.

📄

PRD & Feature Specs

I feed Claude the problem statement, user research snippets, and constraints, then ask it to draft a full PRD including background, goals, non-goals, requirements, and open questions. I refine the output — cutting spec-writing time by 70%.

📋

User Story Writing

I describe a feature in plain English and ask Claude to generate a full set of user stories in INVEST format, including acceptance criteria and edge cases. Engineers get unambiguous tickets; I stop fielding "what did you mean by this?" questions.

📊

Competitor Analysis Briefs

I paste in competitor landing page copy or feature announcements and ask Claude to summarise the positioning, identify differentiation gaps, and suggest counter-moves. A quick scan becomes a structured brief in 10 minutes.

📈

Prioritisation & Roadmap Framing

I use Claude to apply RICE or MoSCoW scoring to a list of features, draft the rationale for each priority decision, and frame the roadmap narrative for leadership. It keeps my reasoning tight and my slides honest.
