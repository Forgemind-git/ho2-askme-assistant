About Me

How I use AI in my teaching

I teach Biology, Chemistry, and Physics to secondary school students across Year 7 to Year 13. AI has become an essential part of how I design lessons, support different learning styles, and keep content fresh.

📋

Lesson Plan Design

I give Claude the curriculum objective, the year group, and the available time, then ask it to draft a lesson plan with a hook, main activity, and exit ticket. I personalise it from there — saving 45 minutes of prep per lesson.

❓

Quiz & Assessment Questions

I ask Claude to generate GCSE-style multiple choice and short-answer questions on any topic, complete with mark schemes. This gives me a question bank I can mix and match for formative assessment.

💡

Plain-English Explainers

When students struggle with a concept like mitosis or electromagnetic induction, I ask Claude to re-explain it using an analogy suited to teenagers. I then read it aloud or display it on the board.

🎯

Differentiation & SEND Support

I use Claude to rewrite worksheets at different reading levels, generate alt-text descriptions for diagrams, and draft scaffolded sentence starters for students who need more structure.
