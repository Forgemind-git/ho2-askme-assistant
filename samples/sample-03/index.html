What does the process look like, and how long does it take?
Three stages: a 1–2 week Discovery Audit, a 3–6 week Design & Build, then handover with 30 days of support. Most small-business engagements wrap up within 6–8 weeks, and you see working pieces every week rather than waiting for one big reveal.
Do you work with very small or early-stage businesses?
Yes — most of my clients are 2–20 people. If you are pre-revenue I will usually point you to a lighter, lower-cost starting package so you do not over-invest in systems before you need them. A quick call is the best way to find out what fits.
