Projects
Kite · Fintech app

Onboarding redesign

A savings app was losing 60% of new users at the KYC step. I simplified it to 3 clear steps, added a progress indicator and rewrote the copy into plain language people actually trust.

Result: activation up ~22%, far fewer KYC drop-offs.

Marketplace · Checkout

Single-page checkout rebuild

A handmade-goods marketplace had complaints about cart abandonment. I rebuilt a confusing 5-step checkout into one accountable page where buyers always know what they're paying and why.

Result: checkout completion up ~15%.

Clinic booking · Design system

Small accessible design system

A health-booking startup's two squads kept diverging on patterns. I built a small, accessible design system so they shared components and language instead of rebuilding them.

Result: screens shipped ~2× faster, WCAG AA throughout.

Field-ops · Research

Dispatch flow research

I ran 12 contextual interviews with delivery drivers to understand the real dispatch experience, then turned the findings into a redesigned flow built around how they actually work.

Result: task time cut by a third.
