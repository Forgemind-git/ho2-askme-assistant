About Me

How I use AI in my data work

I'm a Data Analyst specialising in e-commerce, working across sales funnels, inventory, and customer lifetime value. AI has fundamentally changed how quickly I can move from raw data to actionable insight.

🗄️

SQL Query Generation

I describe the business question in plain English and ask Claude to write the SQL. I then review and tweak it — this cuts my query-writing time by 60% and helps me explore joins I might have missed.

📈

Chart & Dashboard Interpretation

I describe what I see in a chart — the axes, the trend, the anomaly — and ask Claude to hypothesise causes and suggest follow-up analyses. It's like having a second analyst on call.

📝

Executive Summaries

I paste raw metric tables into Claude and ask for a three-paragraph exec summary in plain English. Stakeholders get clear narrative instead of raw numbers — and I write 80% fewer slide decks.

🔍

Anomaly Investigation

When a metric spikes or drops, I feed Claude the context (date, metric, magnitude) and ask it to generate a structured debugging checklist — data quality checks, cohort breakdowns, seasonality flags.
