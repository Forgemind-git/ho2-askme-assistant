About Me

How I use AI in my HR work

I'm an HR Business Partner supporting teams across hiring, performance, and employee relations. AI has become a trusted drafting partner that helps me produce quality documents faster and spend more time on the human side of HR.

📝

Job Description Drafting

I give Claude the role title, team context, and key responsibilities, and it drafts a structured, inclusive JD in minutes. I then refine the tone and add internal salary banding — cutting JD write-up time from 2 hours to 20 minutes.

📄

Policy Document Drafting

From parental leave updates to flexible working policies, I use Claude to produce first drafts that are clear, legally mindful, and written in plain English — not legalese. Legal then reviews; Claude does the heavy lifting first.

🤝

1:1 and Review Prep Notes

Before a performance conversation or difficult 1:1, I ask Claude to help me structure the discussion, frame feedback constructively, and anticipate common responses. It's like a coaching session before the coaching session.

🔍

Interview Question Banks

I ask Claude to generate competency-based interview questions for any role, mapped to our company values and STAR format. Hiring managers get a ready-to-use question pack instead of improvising in the room.
