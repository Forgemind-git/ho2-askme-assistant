# HO2 Sample 1 — Job Seeker

## Your task

You are job-hunting and tired of recruiters skimming past your CV. Build a hosted page with a chat assistant grounded in your skills, projects and goals.

## What to do

1. Open index.html in your browser
2. Edit the placeholder text to match YOUR real information
3. Add your own projects / services / experience
4. Customise the colours and fonts to match your personal brand
5. Push to GitHub and enable Pages for a live shareable link

## Sections to fill in

- Hero (name + tagline)
- About me
- Skills & tools
- Featured projects
- Ask me anything
